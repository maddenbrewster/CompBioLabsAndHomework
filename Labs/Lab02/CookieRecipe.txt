Peanut Butter Blossoms Recipe 

Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup softened butter 
1/2 cup peanut butter 
1 large egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract 
1/4 teaspoon salt 
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda 
1/4 cup additional sugar 
48 Hershey's Kisses (unwrapped)

Directions:
1. Combine 1/2 cup sugar, brown sugar, butter, and peanut butter in bowl. Beat at medium speed until light and fluffy. Add egg, vanilla, and salt; continue beating until well mixed. Add flour and baking soda; beat at low speed, scraping bowl often, until well mixed. Cover with plastic food wrap; refrigerate 30 minutes. 

2. Heat oven to 375 degrees F. 

3. Shape dough into 1 1/4-inch balls. Roll balls in 1/4 cup sugar. Place 2 inches apart onto uncreased cookie sheets. 

4. Bake 8-10 minutes or until very lightly golden brown. Immediately press 1 chocolate kiss in center of each cookie. Place in oven for 10 seconds and remove to cooling racks.
